I have been working on a side-project to generate barcode like image-profiles from entire movies. This particular one is from an movie called [**Coffee run**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PVGeM40dABA) made by Blender Studios. You can also extract color profiles from particular scenes as well. We can see the color palette for this scene from the **Grand Budapest Hotel**.
