## Trip to Milano
### Break during coronavirus lockdown

I had the oppurtunity to travel to Milan during the brief time we had before the second wave of the virus hit Italy. Milan was its usual hub of hustle and bustle, and travelling there from the quaint little town where I live was a welcome pace of change, never had I thought I would miss the ambient noises of Bangalore or Kolkata.
